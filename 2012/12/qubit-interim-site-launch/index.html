QuBit interim site launch

Dec 5, 2012

Yesterday at Big Bite Creative we launched QuBit’s new interim site. It’s built on Less CSS, HTML5 and utilises jQuery to create a modern, slick one-page website. Check it out at: here at it's new location (interim site moved due to Qubit's new site having been launched).

Yesterday we launched a new interim website for QuBit, aligned with the announcement of a $7.5 million investment by Balderton Capital.

QuBit was founded by 4 ex Googlers and is a fast growing technology solutions company. They use big data to understand how users interact with website content and products to find the barriers to conversion and positive experiences. Currently they work with some of the leading eCommerce, publishing and lead generation companies including; BBC, John Lewis, Expedia, Financial Times, Topshop and TalkTalk.

This new site is an interim solution that supports the launch of QuBit’s new larger SaaS analytics platform. Take a look at it here.

We have worked with QuBit over the last 9 months and have previously launched the QuBit products and OpenTag website.

There are lots of exciting things going on at QuBit are we look forward to working with them in 2013.
